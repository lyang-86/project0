Marble Bundt Cake Recipe

Ingredient Amount
Cake Flour 3 cups
Baking Powder 2 tsp
Unsalted Butter 1 cup
Sour Cream/Greek Yogurt 1/2 cup
Granulated Sugar 2 cups
Large Eggs 5 eggs
Milk 3/4 cup
Cocoa Powder 1/3 cup

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350F (180C)
  2. Combine cake flour and baking powder and sift
  3. In a separate bowl, mix the butter and sour cream/greek yogurt
  4. Add sugar into the butter and sour cream/greek yogurt
  5. One by one, add in eggs
  6. Mix in the flour and baking powder, as well as a 1/2 cup of milk
  7. Separate batter into two bowls
  8. Mix in cocoa powder and the remaining 1/4 cup of milk to one bowl of batter
  9. Pour the batter into the bundt cake pan, alternating between the chocolate and vanilla batter
  10. Bake at 350F (180C) for 50-60 mins
  11. Once baked, let cool and top with powdered sugar/frosting of your choice
